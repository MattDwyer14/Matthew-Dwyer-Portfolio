Achievements

  • The morning after graduation I set off on a 1,100-mile self-supported bikepacking trip across the length of the UK, from John O’Groats in Scotland to Land’s End in Cornwall. Carrying my tent and sleeping bag on my pack, I raised £900 in support of the Elsie Normington Foundation.
  • During University I was Education Rep for all Master’s students in the School of Physics and I worked closely with senior members of the department to develop a new Quantum Computing/Technology Master’s program.
  • Was awarded the Thomas Farr Prize from the University of Nottingham for contributions to the School of Physics and Astronomy whilst serving as President of the Physics Society.
